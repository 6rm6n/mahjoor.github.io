Displaced

Vocals by arman and kaedefall, produced by yugen and lb66

arman Â· displaced +kaedefall (yugen + lb66)

This song is definitely a banger, being the most played song I've vocalized on so far. This was the first song I created with kaedefall, one of my favorite creatives and someone I am honored to partner with on several musical compositions. To tell the full story about this song, I'll have to refer to the original piece, which was recorded on a beat by yugen and sense (@176s).

I was in a discord server created by artist Angelus, where the producer Yugen shared a beat file. Upon hearing the beat drop, I downloaded it and imported it into FL Studio. The initial lines flowed out effortlessly.

This was the original version of the song.

yugen had other plans for the beat however, but decided on flipping the vocals that Kaedefall and I created. That is where they collaborated with lb66 on the dynamic, ecstatic, electric beat you hear accompanying our vocals.
